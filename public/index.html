Which type of wood is best for exterior doors?
For exterior doors in the Telangana climate, we recommend Burma Teak or African Teak. Both have high natural oil content, excellent weather resistance, and exceptional durability. Indian Teak is also a great option at a slightly lower price point.
Do you offer delivery to nearby areas?
Yes! We deliver to Shadnagar, Rangareddy, Jadcherla, Mahbubnagar, Kothur, Hyderabad, and surrounding areas. Delivery charges depend on the distance and quantity ordered. Contact us for a delivery quote.
What is WPC and why should I consider it?
WPC (Wood-Plastic Composite) is an engineered material that combines wood fiber and plastic. It is 100% waterproof, termite-proof, and requires zero maintenance. It's ideal for bathrooms, kitchens, and areas exposed to moisture. Cost-wise it's competitive with solid wood doors.
Can I place bulk orders for apartment or commercial projects?
Absolutely! We specialize in bulk supply for builders, contractors, and developers. We have a large inventory and can fulfill large orders consistently. We offer special bulk pricing and phased delivery schedules to suit your project timeline.

Do you do custom sizing and cutting?
Yes, we offer precision custom cutting at our depot. Share your exact dimensions and specifications and we'll cut and prepare the timber to your requirements. Additional cutting charges may apply based on complexity.
